Kingdom 134 rules

The courage to apologize is not shame, but honor.

1. The rulers

1.1. The rulers are the King who holds King's Landing and the leaders of the alliances that hold one of the seven other major castles (Storm's End, Sunspear Riverrun, Highgarden, Eyrie, Casterly Rock and Winterfell) and are therefore considered allies of the kingdom.

1.2. The rulers meet in a chat group with three of their officials who are able to communicate in order to establish collaboration between the various allies of the kingdom.

2. The territories

2.1. Territories are entrusted by the King to allied alliances.

2.2. The territory of each allied alliance corresponds to the region of the major castle it holds and the other minor castles entrusted to it.

2.3. For alliances that do not hold a major castle the 100 Kilometre rule is in force.

3. The hives

3.1. A hive may not be created on the territory of another ally unless authorised by the leader holding the territory.

3.2. An ally's hive or hives that are formed on its territory may not be attacked.

3.3. Hives which are formed on neutral territory may be attacked at any time. That is why it is recommended to join a major alliance or to merge with other alliances in neutral territories to pretend to hold a major castle that would be available.

3.4. When an alliance migrates its hive to another territory it cannot be attacked for 3 days.

4. Isolated castles

4.1. An isolated castle can be attacked all the time.

4.2. An isolated castle of an allied alliance cannot be attacked if it is on its own territory.

5. Resources

5.1. Allied alliances are guaranteed exclusivity of resources within their territory.

5.2. In the case of resource harvesting in neutral territory, the one that starts first has priority.

5.3. Harvesting resources on the territory of another alliance is prohibited.

5.4. Allies of an alliance are free to attack those who try to harvest resources on their territory.

5.5. Under no circumstances is it allowed to attack a harvesting unit on a harvesting site outside of the above rules.

6. The buffs

6.1. All allies of the kingdom are eligible for buffs.

6.2. The number of players from an allied alliance who are eligible to receive buffs is determined by the alliance's KVK ranking.

6.3. Players who are eligible to receive buffs may obtain buffs, in accordance with the handbook, for construction, research and troop training.

Buffs handbook

PO is the person in charge of allocating buffs to the players.

PO announces that it is available by writing "PO on" and that it is no longer available by writing "PO off".

You can only request a buff when PO is available.

The system operates according to a protocol and codification.

For example, before starting the construction of a building, you go to the chat group named "Buffroom" and if PO is available, you write "B+ please".

When PO has assigned you the buff, you will see your name scroll on the screen (as name scrolling can be out of sync, instantly after buff is assigned to you, there is a mail about it, so better watch for the new mail, you can check also your profile to verify the buff is there and correct one) and you can then start the construction of your building.

As soon as it is done, you return to the chat group named "Buffroom" and you write "B- done, thank you" in order to close your request.

It's each one's turn and it's the same protocol for requests for research and for troop training, only the codification changes.

The codes are:

1. B+ and B- for the building construction buff

2. R+ and R- for the research buff

3. T+ and T- for the troops training buff

7. Free attack events

7.1. Free Attack Day, which takes place every Sunday (GMT 0:00 to 23:59), and Lord of Lords Phase 6 are free attack events.

7.2. Rulers may decide to cancel a Free Attack Day event.

7.3. For Free Attack Day, the rulers may prohibit participation by any part of the players based on strength or t4 possession.

7.4. Hives may be attacked but not harvest sites.

8. KVK

8.1. 30 minutes before the start of KVK, it is possible to attack castles that have not activated a truce (GMT 0:00 to 0:30).

8.2. Diamonds are harvested according to the same rules as other resources.

9. Decent behavior

9.1. You must be respectful to all players on the server.

9.2. Insults, abuse against another player are prohibited.

9.3. keep the world chat clean from any personal attacks, politics or discriminatory language

10. Violation of the rules

10.1. In case of rule violation by a player, he will first get a warning and the option for him to apologize. If not, the first sanction is to give him a debuff, and if he persists the second sanction will be to be declared a public enemy.

10.2. In case of rule violation by an alliance (more than 3 alliance members who are public enemy) the alliance is declared public enemy.

10.3. Public enemies can be attacked at any time.
