Kingdom 134 rules

1. The rulers

1.1. The rulers are the King who holds King's Landing and the leaders of the alliances that hold one of the seven other major castles (Storm's End, Sunspear, Riverrun, Highgarden, Eyrie, Casterly Rock and Winterfell) : they are all thus concidered as allies of the kingdom K134.

1.2. The rulers are represented in the 134Leaders chat group by the leader of each allied alliance along with three of their officials who are able to communicate.

1.3. The 134Leaders chat group is a neutral space where the rulers discuss, propose and decide on changes in rules, military strategies and any other matters that need to be brought to the attention of all allies.

1.4. The King is the final decision-maker on the topics discussed.

2. Territories

2.1. Territories are conquered by alliances during the Castle Siege or are given by the King to allied alliances.

2.2. The King's allies may only hold one major castle.

2.3. The territory of each allied alliance corresponds to the regions of the major castles it holds and the other minor castles it is entrusted with.

2.4. For alliances that do not hold a major castle the 100 Kilometre rule is in force.

3. Hives

3.1. At least ten allies of an alliance must be next to each other to be recognized as a hive.

3.2. A hive may not be created on the territory of another ally unless authorised by the ruler holding the territory.

3.3. An ally's hive or hives formed on its territory may not be attacked.

3.4. Hives which are formed on neutral territory may be attacked at any time. Therefore, it is recommended to join a major alliance or merge with other alliances in neutral territories to claim to hold a major castle that would be available.

3.5. An isolated castle can be attacked at any time, but an isolated castle of an allied alliance cannot be attacked if it is on its own territory or if it is on the territory of another alliance with which there is a non-aggression pact (NAP).

4. Resources

4.1. The alliance is guaranteed the exclusivity to collect resources from the occupied territory.

4.2 Harvesting resources on the territory of another alliance is forbidden.

4.3. Allies of an alliance are free to attack those who try to harvest resources on their territory.

4.4. In the case of resource harvesting in neutral territory, the one who starts first has priority.

4.5. Under no circumstances is it allowed to attack a harvesting unit on a harvesting site outside of the above rules.
