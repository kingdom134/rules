TTB rules

Communication is highly appreciated and the same goes for participation in events. Relocation to the hive is mandatory.

2. participate in trials (elite + alliance)

3. participate in events (AC, AM, KvK, AoD, CS if there is competitor to our Great Castle etc.) if can't just notify teammates

4. there are few more rules but they are about specific event and they are mailed right before and after the event

5. read alliance mail messages

5.1. alliance-tavern chat (especially if you are tagged)

5.2. market chat (especially if you are tagged)

>6. do not use world chat, especially if there is problem, contact Alliance officers so they can solve the problem for you

6.1. Do not blame-insult other players

6.2. Do not enter in conflicts or explanations where you tries to prove that you are right... etc.

8. use the Banker, if your resources are above your limit or no bubble (shield), please donate to the Banker, you can always request resources back when need them (use Market for anything related to ressources)

9. if need to do Rebel Camp not available in TTB territories but got it in other Alliance territories please ask Alliance officers to request for permission from other Alliance to attack their Rebel Camp

10. if rename your lord please send alliance message-mail so teammates will know about the change

11. if anything is not clear or missing, please do not haste to ask in Alliance-Tarver chat

11.1. if do not know anything about specific aspect of the game or facing specific dificulties

11.2. if some rule (alliance or kingdom) is not clear or think that it can be improved
